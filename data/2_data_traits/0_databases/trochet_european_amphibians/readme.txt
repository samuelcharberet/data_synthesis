Trochet 2014 - European Amphibians Database

Amphibian life history traits from literature in an extensive database with morphological and behavioral traits, 
habitat preferences and movement abilities for 86 European amphibian species (50 Anuran and 36 Urodela species).
Body_weight , Male_body_length , Female_body_length , Body_length , Foot length , Tibia length , Hind limb length , 
Metamorphosis size , Number of eggs , Survival rates in adults , Sexual maturity , Movement ability.

Data available at:
https://doi.org/10.3897/BDJ.2.e4123
