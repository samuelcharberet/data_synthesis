Andrieux 2020 - Body stoichiometry of heterotrophs: assessing drivers of interspecific variations in elemental composition

17848 independent observations on 1491 species of amphibians, fishes (Euteleosteomorpha and Otomorpha), 
invertebrates, mammals, microbes and sauropsids (birds and reptiles).
