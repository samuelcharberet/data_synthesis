De Swardt 2018 - Insect outbreaks alter nutrient dynamics in a southern African savanna: patchy defoliation of Colophospermum mopane savanna by Imbrasia belina larvae

C, N, P, K, as well as other elements in leaves, body, and frass of lepidopterans worms in African savannah.
Raw data were retrieved from the dryad repository
https://datadryad.org/stash/dataset/doi:10.5061/dryad.p725tq4

The R document 1234_001_deswardt_2018_data_wrangling.r wrangles this raw data and gives averages for the 13 elements in food, body and frass of insects.
