Valdès-Correcher 2019 - Herbivore dung quality affects plant community diversity

CNP composition of faeces of 11 European herbivores collected in 3 nature reserves (the Netherlands and Belgium), in winter and in spring.
Testing of the effect of the quality and quantity of faeces of 5 of the 11 species brought to a community of plants in mesocosms.
