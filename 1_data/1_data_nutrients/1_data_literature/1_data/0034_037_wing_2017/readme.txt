Wing 2017 - Marine micronutrient vectors: seabirds, marine mammals and fishes egest high concentrations of
bioactive metals in the subantarctic island ecosystem

Fe, Co, Mn, Zn, Ni, Cu, As, Cd (ICP-MS) in guano from 8 species of seabird: southern black-backed gull (Larus
dominicanus), sooty shearwater (Ardenna grisea), redbilled gull (Larus novaehollandiae), cape pigeon (Daption
capense), Antarctic tern (Sterna vittata), Snares crested penguin (Eudyptes robustus), brown skua (Stercorarius
antarctica) and Buller’s albatross (Thalassarche bulleri) and faeces of Hooker’s sea lions (Phocarctos
hookeri) and New Zealand fur seals (Arctocephalus forsteri).
