Pacyna 2019 - Evaluation of the use of reindeer droppings for monitoring essential and non-essential elements in the polar terrestrial environment

In this paper, the droppings of Svalbard reindeer were collected to examine the excretion pathway of 30 elements.
Samples were collected in mesic and moss tundra, representing summer, winter and winter-transitional excretion.

Ca, Fe, K, Mg, P, S, and Si using ICP-OES
Ag, Al, As, B, Ba, Be, Cd, Co, Cr, Cu, La, Li, Mn, Mo, Na, Ni, Pb, Sb, Se, Sr, V, and Zn using ICP-MS
